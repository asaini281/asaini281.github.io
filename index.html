Semantic Segmentation of Road Scenes (Deep Learning Project)

Developed and trained CNN-based models (Simple CNN & U-Net) for semantic segmentation on the KITTI Road dataset using TensorFlow/Keras. Implemented custom training loops with TensorBoard integration for monitoring loss, accuracy, and visualization of predicted masks. Optimized data pipelines, applied augmentation, and evaluated results using metrics like IoU and Dice coefficient. Achieved pixel-level classification of road vs. background, enabling applications in autonomous driving and scene understanding.

Earthquake Data Analysis: Seismic Trends & Impact Assessment

Conducted an in-depth statistical analysis using R to study global earthquake data. Analyzed patterns in magnitude, frequency, and geographical distribution of earthquakes, as well as their correlation with population density and infrastructure exposure. Employed data cleaning, time-series analysis, clustering techniques, and regression models to identify high-risk regions and long-term seismic trends. Developed interactive visualizations and dashboards to communicate insights on earthquake impact and risk assessment.

Social Game Systems (SGS)

Developed and presented a game model, Social Game System (SGS), designed for a dynamic universe of players with structured, reflexive, and symmetrical acquaintance relations. The model introduces player neighborhoods with fixed positions, emphasizing flexible connections and interaction limits. Focused on designing scalable, numerable systems and analyzing social dynamics within constrained environments.
